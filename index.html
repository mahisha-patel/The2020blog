Era of Social Distancing
and Social Networking


With a large number of the world’s population now confined at home, people have turned to social media as an outlet to remain connected with family, friends, and work.

  • Social networking: a key in social distancing.
  • Social distancing: a key to survive this pandemic.

Some measures to be taken care of in this situation:

Wear Mask

Wear mask to prevent the disease enter your body through your mouth and nose.

Wash Hands

To prevent virus to enter through body transmission, wash your hands frequently.

Keep social distance

Keep safe distance from everyone you meet while going outside and also at your home.

Wear Gloves

Wear gloves to avoid touch while going outside and meeting new people.

Stay home

Unless it is urgent and necessary, stay home and try to avoid going out.

Don't touch your face

Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.

Good Deeds

Good Deeds

In this critical situation, here are some good deeds done this year.

Helping migrants/labours in India

Sonu Sood has taken it upon himself to unite migrant workers with their families and make them reach their homes safely amid the ongoing Covid-19 crisis and nationwide lockdown. And his relentless efforts have earned him admiration and adulation from all parts of the country. More so because his endeavour isn’t just limited to Maharashtra, as he has been getting requests from all over India.
Infact not just him,there are many such people who have done social service in this situation and he is just one of them.

US announced $174 mn aid to 64 countries

The United States announced USD 174 million financial assistance to 64 countries including USD 2.9 million to India to help them fight the coronavirus pandemic.
This is in addition to the USD 100 million aid announced by the US in February.
The newly announced assistance is part of a larger American global response package across multiple departments and agencies, including the Centres for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). The funding is for 64 of the most at-risk countries facing the threat of the global pandemic.
